Our Mission

JoyCentric Organization's mission is to reach out to and support all oncology patients.

This is accomplished through various fashions such as:

  • meeting and connecting with patients
  • assembling and distributing handmade gift bags
  • hosting social events and gatherings
  • fundraising for donations
